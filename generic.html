Designed a Well-Architected, multi-AZ platform for business-critical workloads with secure edge delivery, scalable compute, and DR-ready design.

  • Database layer using managed patterns where possible

  • Operational readiness: logging, monitoring, and incident response workflows

Services & Practices

  • EC2, ALB, RDS, S3, CloudFront

Outcome

  • Improved availability and scalability with Multi-AZ foundation
  • Stronger security posture with WAF, IAM controls, and audit logging
